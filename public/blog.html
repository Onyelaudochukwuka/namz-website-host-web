30 Days Of


Web Development

I am Namrutha Hari, a first year Computer Engineering Student. One day, when I was scrolling across Instagram feed, a poster caught my attention. It was a poster about '30 days of Web Development' by DSC Hits. I was amused since I didn't think it was possible to learn so many things just within the timeline of thirty days. Anyhow, I registered for the event since I wanted to learn HTML and CSS.

The program was hard to understand at the initial days since it involved so many steps to be followed with the Skillenza platform and github repository. But as days passed, the steps were easier to follow. I would wait each day for the tasks the facilitators had to assign.

I am someone who loves challenges. The deadline for each project pushed me to complete learning the entire content on time and complete the projects. The mentors really created the content in a very well organised way. Any beginner can self learn form these materials. I am very grateful that I tried out this program. I gained so much information in such a short time.
